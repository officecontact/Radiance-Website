Active Certifications

1

APEDA Registered

Agricultural and Processed Food Products Export Development Authority. Mandatory registration for agricultural exporters in India. Reg. No. on request.

Export Compliance
2

India Organic (NPOP)

National Programme for Organic Production — India's national organic standard. Equivalent to EU Organic under the India-EU organic equivalency agreement.

Organic Certification
3

EU Organic

Compliant with EU Council Regulation (EC) No 834/2007 for organic production. All products destined for EU markets carry EU Organic certification documentation.

EU Market Access
4

USDA NOP Organic

United States Department of Agriculture National Organic Program. Certified organic for import into the United States. Certified via accredited certifying body.

USA Market Access
5

Canada Organic (COR)

Canadian Organic Regime certification. Enables direct import to Canada as certified organic product under CFIA regulations. Issued by accredited certifying body.

Canada Market Access
6

GMP+ Certified

GMP+ Feed Safety Assurance certification for animal feed products. Mandatory for animal feed export to EU countries under EU Feed Hygiene Regulation.

Feed Safety
7

ISO 9001:2015

International quality management standard ISO 9001:2015. Ensures consistent quality management systems across sourcing, processing, documentation and export operations.

Quality Management
8

FIEO Member

Federation of Indian Export Organisations member. India's apex body for exporters — membership ensures recognition and access to government export promotion schemes.

Trade Body

Documents Provided with Every Shipment

Complete documentation package — no delays at customs.

📋 Commercial Invoice & Packing List

Detailed invoice with HS codes, quantity, unit value, and complete packing list with container loading details.

🌿 Phytosanitary Certificate

Issued by Plant Quarantine Authority of India. Mandatory for agricultural product exports. Confirms product is free from quarantine pests.

🏆 APEDA Certificate of Origin

Official APEDA-issued CoO confirming product origin as India. Required for preferential tariff treatment under trade agreements.

🔬 NABL Certificate of Analysis

Full lab report from NABL-accredited lab — pesticide residues (MRL compliance), heavy metals, mycotoxins, aflatoxin, microbiological tests.

🌿 Organic Transaction Certificate

Transaction Certificate (TC) from accredited organic certifier. Required by EU, USA, and Canada for import of organic products.

🚢 Fumigation Certificate

ISPM-15 compliant fumigation certificate (methyl bromide / aluminium phosphide). Required by most countries for grain and agro shipments.
