Frequently Asked Questions — Oil Seeds

Radiance Overseas exports a comprehensive range of Oil Seeds products sourced directly from certified farms and processing units across India. All products are FSSAI compliant and available in custom grades and packaging.

Our Oil Seeds products carry the following certifications: IOPEPC, EU Organic, FSSAI, NABL Tested. These ensure compliance with international food safety and quality standards.

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ) for Oil Seeds starts at 5 MT. Trial shipments in smaller quantities are available at a slight premium for new buyers.

Standard packaging includes 25 kg and 50 kg PP woven bags, 1–5 kg consumer packs, and bulk container shipments (20'/40' FCL). Custom private-label packaging is also available.

We provide Phytosanitary Certificate, Certificate of Analysis (COA), Certificate of Origin (CoO), FSSAI compliance document, and any market-specific documentation required by the importing country.
