Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity for export? +
For most products, the minimum order quantity is 1 × 20ft FCL (approximately 20–25 MT depending on product). For some specialty products (e.g., cardamom, herbs), minimum is 500kg–1 MT. Contact us with your requirement for a specific MOQ confirmation.
What certifications do you provide with shipments? +
We provide: APEDA Certificate of Origin, Phytosanitary Certificate, Fumigation Certificate, NABL CoA (Certificate of Analysis), India Organic / EU Organic / USDA NOP organic certificates, SGS Inspection Report on request, and any country-specific certificates required.
Do you offer private label / custom packaging? +
Yes. We offer full private label packaging — from 500g retail pouches to 50kg bulk bags. Your logo, design, and specifications. MOQ for private label varies by product — typically 1–5 MT. Contact us with your packaging brief.
What are your payment terms? +
For new customers: 30% advance + 70% against BL (Bill of Lading) copy. For regular customers: LC at sight or DP terms. We work with SWIFT, FIRC, and all standard international banking instruments.
How long does shipping take from India? +
Transit times vary by destination: UAE/Gulf 5–10 days, Europe 18–25 days, USA/Canada 22–30 days, Africa 15–25 days, Southeast Asia 12–18 days. All from JNPT (Nhava Sheva) or Mundra port. We provide pre-shipment samples upon request.
