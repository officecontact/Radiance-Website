Frequently Asked Questions

What organic products does Radiance Overseas export from India?+
We export 169+ certified organic products across 11 categories: Indian Rice (Basmati & Non-Basmati), Spices, Oil Seeds, Pulses & Dal, Coffee, Cereals & Flour, Dry Fruits, Dehydrated Vegetables, Herbs & Crude Drugs, Food Chemicals, and Animal Feed. All products comply with EU Organic, India Organic (NPOP) and international food safety standards.
Is Radiance Overseas APEDA registered?+
Yes. We are registered with APEDA, the Spices Board of India, Coffee Board of India, and IOPEPC. We also hold EU Organic, India Organic (NPOP), GMP+, and ISO 9001:2015 certifications — ensuring compliance for all major export markets.
What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ) for Indian organic products?+
MOQ varies by product. Most spices and rice start at 1 MT. Dehydrated products and herbs can be sourced from 100 kg. We accommodate trial orders for new buyers. Contact our export team for precise MOQ and pricing for your destination market.
Which countries does Radiance Overseas export organic products to?+
We export to 30+ countries including the USA, Germany, UK, Netherlands, UAE, Saudi Arabia, Nigeria, Ghana, South Africa, Singapore, Malaysia, and Australia. Our offices in Indore (India), Wolfsburg (Germany), Galway (Ireland) and the USA ensure fast local support.
How do I get a quote for bulk organic product export from India?+
Simply fill our enquiry form on the Contact page or WhatsApp our export team. Mention the product, quantity, and destination country. We respond within 24 hours with pricing, availability, and shipping details.
