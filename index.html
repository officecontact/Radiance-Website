About Radiance Overseas

Bridging India's Organic
Farms to the World

Radiance Overseas is a vertically integrated organic agro-product export house headquartered in Indore, Madhya Pradesh — the commercial heart of Central India. From farm-gate procurement to containerized shipment, we manage every stage of the export value chain in-house: sourcing, grading, processing, lab testing, documentation, and global freight logistics.

Our operations span four countries — India (HQ & sourcing), Germany (EU market desk), Ireland (quality compliance), and the USA (sales & distribution). This on-ground presence across time zones means faster response, smoother customs clearance, and a dedicated relationship manager in your region.

We export across 11 commodity categories — from premium 1121 Basmati rice and single-origin Arabica coffee to high-curcumin turmeric, hulled sesame seeds, freeze-dried instant coffee, kabuli chickpeas, dehydrated onion flakes, ashwagandha root, and food-grade guar gum. Every container leaves port with an NABL-accredited Certificate of Analysis, APEDA Certificate of Origin, Phytosanitary Certificate, and organic Transaction Certificate — so your import clears customs without delay.
