Frequently Asked Questions

What agro products does Radiance Overseas export?
We export 11 categories: rice (Basmati & non-Basmati), spices (whole & powdered), oil seeds (sesame, groundnuts, mustard), pulses (chickpeas, toor dal, moong dal), coffee (Arabica, Robusta, instant), cereals & flour, dry fruits, dehydrated vegetables, herbs & crude drugs, food chemicals, and animal feed. 490+ individual SKUs.
Is Radiance Overseas APEDA registered?
Yes. We are APEDA registered and hold FIEO membership, ISO 9001:2015, USDA NOP Organic, EU Organic (EC 834/2007), Canada Organic (COR), India Organic (NPOP), and GMP certifications.
What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?
Bulk commodities (rice, spices, sesame, pulses): typically 1 FCL = 18–25 MT. Herbs and specialty items: from 100 kg. Dehydrated products: from 500 kg. Mixed-FCL combining multiple products is available.
Does Radiance Overseas supply organic certified products?
Yes. We hold USDA NOP, EU Organic, Canada Organic (COR), and India Organic (NPOP) certifications. Organic variants are available across rice, spices, oil seeds, pulses, cereals, herbs and dehydrated products. Organic Transaction Certificates (TC) and Annex VI certificates provided per shipment.
